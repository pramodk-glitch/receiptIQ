# ReceiptIQ – Quick Overview (Short Version)

🧾 *ReceiptIQ – Quick Overview*

📷 *Core Features:*
• Scan receipts or auto-pull from 20+ retailers via Gmail/Outlook
• AI extracts every line item automatically
• Covers groceries, medicine, electronics, dining, bills & more
• Manual entry option too

💰 *Price Intelligence:*
• Full price history per item across all stores
• Live vendor comparison (Walmart, Target, Amazon, Costco & more)
• Personal inflation tracker vs. CPI

🛒 *Smart Shopping:*
• AI estimates shopping list cost per store
• Suggests optimal store split to save the most
• Budget alerts via push, email & SMS

📊 *Analytics (40+ reports):*
• Spending overview, price intelligence, purchase behavior
• Bills & recurring, health & wellness, savings, forecasting

🤖 *AI Spending Assistant:*
• Ask anything in plain English — "How much did I spend on dining last month?"
• Instant answers with charts, sourced from your actual data

⚡ *Auto-Ingestion:*
• Connect Gmail/Outlook once — receipts flow in automatically
• Forward any receipt to your unique ReceiptIQ email address
• Browser extension pulls Amazon, Costco, Walmart order history
• Retailer API partnerships (Phase 5)

📋 *Bills & Checks:*
• Auto-captures utility bills, subscriptions, restaurant checks, travel
• Weekly digest — tap Keep or Dismiss per item

👨‍👩‍👧 *Advanced:*
• Household/family shared mode
• Barcode scanner, subscription & loyalty tracking
• Export CSV/PDF/Excel + Mint, YNAB, QuickBooks
• FSA/HSA tax reports

📱 *Platforms:* iOS, Android & full web portal

🌍 *International:* Multi-currency, multi-language, GDPR compliant
